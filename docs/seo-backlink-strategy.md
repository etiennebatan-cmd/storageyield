## 10. Forbidden Tactics

### Never Use
- **Paid Links**: No link buying or selling
- **Spam Directories**: Low-quality farm sites
- **Fake Guest Posts**: AI-generated or outsourced content
- **Excessive Reciprocal Links**: More than 20% of backlinks
- **Hidden Links**: Footer or invisible links
- **Anchor Text Spam**: Over-optimized exact match anchors
- **Link Farms**: Networks of interlinked sites
- **Comment Spam**: Forum or blog comment links
- **Widget Links**: Automatic footer widgets
- **Expired Domain Links**: Previously penalized sites

### Quality Standards
- All links must provide genuine value to users
- Content must be original and helpful
- Outreach must be personalized and relevant
- Links must come from authoritative, relevant sites
- Anchor text must be natural and varied
